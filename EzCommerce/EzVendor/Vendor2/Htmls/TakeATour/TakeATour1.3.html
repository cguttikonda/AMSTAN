Here you get Purchase Order Details of the selected Purchase Order i.e -- > Line No, Material Number, Material Description, UOM, Order Qty, Delv. Qty, Price/Unit, Value, Plant (clicking on this hyperlink shows you where the material is expected to be delivered) and the Expected Delivery Date. The buttons in the bottom offers you with navigational choices. Comitted Dates allows you to enter/view the delivery schedule comitted dates. View Shipments allows you to view your past shipments against the order. View Receipts allows you to follow the status of your shipments to Enterprise. Payment Details allows you to view the Invoices and payments associated with this order.
